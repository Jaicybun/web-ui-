FIRST LOOK: Ferrari unveil new SF-24 car ahead of the 2024 season

Ferrari kept the F1 launch season rolling on Tuesday by showing off their eagerly anticipated new challenger in an online presentation.

Following swiftly in the footsteps of Aston Martin, the famous Italian marque virtually unveiled their SF-24 – which continues Ferrari’s recent naming tradition – via a special video and series of images. It unsurprisingly sports another deep red livery and is a car Ferrari hope will mark a return to title contention as they hunt for their first constructors’ championship since 2008 and first drivers’ crown since 2007.
